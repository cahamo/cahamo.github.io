What's cooking?

After a period of online research and ploughing through the (badly written) examples from the Elegoo Uno R3 Basic Starter Kit I was itching to get going. What I came up with for my first project was the ...

Electro Tongue Drum

The what? An electronic version of a steel tongue drum is what.

Why?

One of the projects that atttracted me when browsing around was the paper piano: a little keyboard that's operated by tapping on "keys" drawn on paper and filled in with pencil "lead". The concept of detecting a finger by just touching a graphite pad intrigued me.

Now it's a little known fact that I'm an amateur composer and producer of electronic music, so a musical project appealed. For a while I've been interested in tongue drums. A decent one is a bit pricey for something I'd probably only play rarely, so ... you know what's coming ... let's make an electronic version of a tongue drum, with touch sensitive keys.

It's decided then. My first project is going to be the Electro Tongue Drum. I reckon it's going to take a while, so please check back for updates.
