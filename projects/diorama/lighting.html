Diorama: Model Lighting In progress

Five buildings are planned for the diorama, all of which will be lit.

LEDs will be used for all lighting. Building lights will be emulated using 3mm white LEDs. Open fires and a forge will be emulated using flickering 3mm amber LEDs. So as not to stress the microcontroller's power limits, LEDs will be switched via transistors.

Here are details of the buildings, the number of LEDs required and the progress with construction.

Building Description White LEDs Flicker LEDs Status
Twin bay shed This building is nearly complete, modified from a card kit. It is currently wired with 2 × 3mm white LEDs and a 3mm amber flicker LED inserted in a fireplace. 2 1 In progress
Barn (converted to workshop) This building is under construction from a card kit that will be modified. There is a rear lean-to that has been fitted with 2 × 3mm white LEDs. An office fitted in the main building has a single 3mm white LED. The main structure will be lit with 2 × 3mm white LEDs (that will switch on and off together) and there will be a forge, lit with a 3mm amber flicker LED. 5 1 In progress
Lean-to shed (TBC) The shed will be a small lean-to building set against a high wall. It will have a single overhead white LED light and a flicker LED in the chimney breast. The lean-to has been designed but not yet built. 1 1 In progress
Large shed / workshop (TBC) This building will possibly have a single light and a stove / fireplace. 1 1 Not started
Site office A plastic kit has been obtained, largely built, and modified to take LEDs. The office is lit with a single 3mm white LED and a 3mm amber flicker LED to represent the open fire. 1 1 In progress

Design

The lights are so numerous that some form of multiplexing will be required.

Progress

An experiment has been conducted to try to find the optimum method to use to multiplex the LEDs.
