Disastrous Kit Builds!

Not all kit builds worked out well. Maybe I screwed up or maybe the kit was faulty. There's a brief rundown of the ones that got away on the pages listed below.

4 Digit Electronic Clock Kit

A kit to construct a 4 digit alarm clock.

Line Tracking Car Kit

This kit contains parts to construct a robot car that follows a simple track, provided with the kit.

MERG Arc Flash Generator Kit

This kit contains parts to construct a simulation of the arcing produced by electric railway locomotive pickup shoes.
